SmartTorch, SmarTorch | About

"SmartTorch" and "SmarTorch" are both intelligent flashlight apps that put you in control. Light up your surroundings with a simple tap, and set a timer to automatically turn off the light when you don't need it anymore.

Falling asleep with the flashlight on: Set a timer to turn off the flashlight after a certain time, helping you ensure that you don't wake up with a drained battery.

Wanna read in the dark?: Use the timer to turn off the flashlight after you finish reading, preventing unnecessary battery usage.

Why Choose SmartTorch? Convenient and Reliable: Simple, user-friendly interface with powerful features.

Customizable: Tailor your flashlight experience with flexible timer options.

Versatile: Perfect for a variety of situations, from everyday tasks to emergencies.
