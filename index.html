While we strive for a seamless experience, you acknowledge and agree to the following technical realities of the Android No Fault Clause:
If the flashlight fails to respond to a command ecosystem:

System Overrides:
The flashlight hardware is managed by the Android System (OS) and the Camera2 API.
In certain instances, the Android System may prevent the flashlight from turning off or on due to resource conflicts, battery optimization, do not disturb being turned on, or background processes.

Third-Party Interference:
Other apps with camera permissions may "lock" the hardware, preventing SmartTorch from executing a "Power Off" command successfully.
